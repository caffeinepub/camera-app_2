# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Ensure AI noise reduction for photos and video in low-light conditions is fully functional and properly integrated across the application.

**Planned changes:**
- Verify the `useNoiseReduction` hook is correctly wired and functioning
- Confirm photo pipeline integration applies noise reduction in low-light, Night, and Portrait modes
- Confirm video pipeline integration applies noise reduction in low-light, Night, and Portrait modes
- Verify the AI Denoise toggle in the UI correctly enables/disables the noise reduction feature

**User-visible outcome:** Users can capture photos and video in low-light conditions with AI-powered noise reduction applied automatically or via the AI Denoise toggle, resulting in cleaner, less noisy output.
